T-shirt Design Contest

11/6/2017

Attention all NHS members: the officers of NHS are holding a T-shirt design contest. Complete this form to submit your design, and the best design will be used for the 2017-2018 NHS shirt!

Here are some requirements:

Use this site to create a design OR the following parameters if using a different program because we will be using M & J for our printing. Use up to 3 colors. Keep in mind that these will be printed on short sleeved unisex t-shirts.

Image Parameters:

  • TIFF, BMP, JPG or other Raster images that are 1-Bit black and white camera ready artwork. No Grayscale. Should be in a minimum of 300 DPI resolution.
  • CorelDraw, Adobe Illustrator, EPS, PDF vector artwork that has all text converted to curves or outlines.
  • We accept both PC and MAC artwork files.
Unacceptable Files:
  • JPG or GIF images or other low resolution images converted to a TIFF or BMP and even if the resolution is changed to 300 dpi, are not acceptable if the original image was less than 300 DPI. Simply resaving a file as a new file type, does not increase the quality of the pixels in the artwork itself. Images from web sites are typically in 72 or 96 DPI.
  • Do not embed image files inside of word documents or other documents such as spreadsheets, rather provide the raw artwork file itself.

Food Drive

9/9/2017

As an organization dedicated to improving our community through service, we recognize that communities hit the hardest by Hurricane Harvey and Hurricane Irma need our assistance.

That is why we need YOUR help! Bring non-perishable food items in your first hour class or room I202 for families in need in Houston and Florida.

For every 5 items you will receive 1 hour. You can earn a maximum of 5 hours through this drive, but don't let this stop you from bringing in more!
